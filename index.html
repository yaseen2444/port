My Projects

Shrimp-Seed-Counter

This project uses a Faster R-CNN with a ResNet backbone to detect prawn seeds in images for aquaculture. It integrates region proposal networks and SAHI to enhance accuracy, especially for small objects. Ideal for automating prawn-seed detection with high precision.

Book-recommender

This project leverages machine learning to build a Book Recommendation System that suggests books based on user preferences. It utilizes collaborative filtering and content-based filtering to provide personalized recommendations. The system analyzes user interactions, book genres, and ratings to enhance recommendation accuracy, helping readers discover books tailored to their interests.

RAG-Matrix

Multi-RAG Builder is an advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system that lets users upload PDFs to create unique AI-powered chat applications. Each RAG app has a unique ID, enabling multiple users to chat with their respective document knowledge bases efficiently.🚀

BlogForge AI

BlogForge AI is an AI-powered blog and documentation generator that helps professionals create engaging, well-structured, and SEO-friendly content with ease. Whether you need a technical tutorial, project documentation, or a general blog post, BlogForge AI leverages Google's Gemini AI to generate high-quality markdown-formatted content. It also includes fact-checking capabilities, markdown export, and an option to publish directly on Medium. 🚀

Smart Promo Background AI

This project combines AI-powered background generation with promotional text creation for product images. It utilizes a diffusion-based model to generate backgrounds and the Gemini API to analyze images and generate promotional text. The system then creates different promotional layouts, ensuring visually appealing product advertisements.

My Blogs

“Bridging the Gap: Can AI Conversations Feel Truly Human?”

Human conversations have a natural ebb and flow, with real-time interruptions, clarifications, and adjustments. However, AI interactions often lack this flexibility, as responses are generated linearly without interruption. This article explores the challenges of making Large Language Models (LLMs) more conversationally dynamic, potential solutions, and their effectiveness in mimicking human-like interactions.

“Edge AI Model Optimization: Making Deep Learning Faster, Smaller & More Efficient”.

Deploying deep learning models on edge devices like smartphones and IoT systems poses challenges due to limited processing power, memory, and battery life. This article explores key optimization techniques—quantization, knowledge distillation, and pruning—that enhance model efficiency while maintaining accuracy. Learn how to apply these methods to make AI solutions faster, smaller, and more suitable for real-world edge applications.

““Building a RAG Application from Scratch: Step-by-Step””

This article provides a step-by-step guide to building a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) application from scratch. Learn how to integrate retrieval-based techniques with generative AI to enhance response accuracy, optimize performance, and create more context-aware applications. Perfect for developers looking to implement RAG in real-world scenarios.

“The Blind Spots of AI Image Generation: My Surprising Discoveries with DALL-E”

This article explores the hidden limitations of AI image generation, based on firsthand experiences with DALL-E. It delves into biases, inconsistencies, and unexpected results that arise when generating images, shedding light on the challenges and surprises of working with AI-driven creativity.

“Automated Shrimp Seed Counting with Deep Learning and SAHI: A Smart Aquaculture Solution”

This article presents an AI-powered approach to automate shrimp seed counting using deep learning and SAHI (Slicing Aided Hyper Inference). It explores model selection, dataset preparation, and optimization techniques to improve accuracy in real-world aquaculture settings, enhancing efficiency and reducing manual effort.
